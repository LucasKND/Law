Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What are questions to ask a lawyer?

When meeting with a lawyer, consider asking about:

  • Their experience with cases similar to yours
  • The potential outcomes of your case
  • Their fee structure and payment options
  • How they communicate with clients
  • Timeline expectations for your case
  • The strengths and weaknesses of your case

These questions will help you understand what to expect and determine if the lawyer is the right fit for your needs.

Q2. How can I install this theme?

Installing this theme is simple:

  1. Download the theme package from your account
  2. Log into your WordPress admin panel
  3. Go to Appearance > Themes
  4. Click "Add New" then "Upload Theme"
  5. Choose the downloaded theme file and click "Install Now"
  6. Once installed, click "Activate"

After activation, you'll find the theme settings under Appearance > Theme Options.

Q3. How can I make it copy interior?

To copy the interior design of any demo:

  1. Navigate to the "Demo Import" section in the theme settings
  2. Choose the demo version you want to replicate
  3. Select "Import Content" to bring in all elements
  4. Wait for the import process to complete
  5. Customize colors, fonts, and images as needed

You can also import specific pages or sections using the "Import Parts" feature.
